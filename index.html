What We Do

Our Products & Services

We specialize in manufacturing capital equipment and engineering solutions tailored to the most demanding industrial needs.

SS Tanks

Hygienic stainless steel tanks engineered for durable, contamination-free storage across food, chemical, and industrial applications.

Silos

High-capacity bulk storage silos for grains, cement, and industrial materials — designed for durability and easy discharge.

RO Plants

Reverse osmosis systems purifying water to the highest standards for drinking, industrial, and commercial applications.

Pressure Vessels

ASME-standard certified pressure vessels for compressed gases and fluids — built for safety under extreme operational conditions.

Steam Boilers

Heavy-duty industrial boilers for steam generation and process heating, built with precision for maximum thermal efficiency.

Pipeline Works

End-to-end pipeline fabrication, installation, and on-site commissioning for oil, gas, water, and chemical transport systems.

Water Treatment

Complete wastewater and industrial water treatment systems — designed, fabricated, and delivered turnkey.

Diesel Tanks

Safe, leak-proof diesel fuel storage tanks for generators and industrial setups — available in standard and bespoke sizes.

Our Story

Journey & Collaboration

1998

Founded in Sharjah, UAE

OSA Engineering established its first fabrication facility in the UAE with a vision to serve the growing industrial demands of the Middle East with world-class precision.

2015

Expansion to India & Saudi Arabia

Opened branch offices in Madurai, India and Ras Tanura, Saudi Arabia — enabling faster delivery and on-ground project support across the region.

2024

Connect Souq Expo, Dubai

Proudly participated in the Connect Souq Expo, a premier Muslim business networking event, showcasing our advanced tank solutions and engineering capabilities to a global audience.

2025

Rifa Business Meet — Tanzania & Dubai

Represented OSA Engineering at Rifa Meets in Tanzania and Dubai, building strategic business ties across African markets and receiving awards for outstanding sponsorship and contribution.
